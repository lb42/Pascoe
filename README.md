# All about Charles Eyre Pascoe (1842-1912) 
This repository contains 
 - A bibliography of CEP's works
 - An account of his life
 - A TEI transcription of his Dramatic List (in progress)
 - A selection of his letters to the press
 - A selection of reviews of his works
 - His obituary and other press reports
   
 These are all TEI XML documents, which you are free to download and repurpose as you wish. If you just want to read then, please visit the Pascoe Portal at https://lb42.github.io/Pascoe, which uses CETEICEAN to make them readable in your browser.

Most of Pascoe's published work is freely available from Hathi Trust and other digital libraries as PDF page images. Links to these resources are included in the bibliography file.  For titles not thus available, the bibliography also includes shelf marks from the Bodleian, the British Library, or the National Library of Scotland's collections.

The folder WIP in this repository contains working documents not yet completely processed.
