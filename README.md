# Pascoe
All about Charles Eyre Pascoe (1842-1912) 
This repository contains 
 - A bibliography of CEP's works
 - An account of his life
 - A TEI transcription of his Dramatic List (in progress)
 - Working documents not yet completely processed
 - A selection of his letters to the press
 - A selection of reviews of his works
 - His obituary and other press reports  

Most of Pascoe's works are freely available for download from the Hathi Trust, and the bibliography includes links to these.
